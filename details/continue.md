# Continue

Continue is an open-source IDE autopilot connecting any LLM (local or cloud) for completions, chat, and edits in VS Code/JetBrains with codebase indexing. Customizable slash commands and diff previews enhance workflow. In 2026, it's the top Ollama companion, flexible unlike locked-in Copilot.
