## Overview

Automates PR documentation with AI-generated guides.

## Features

- Diff-based change summaries and visuals.
- Risk detection and improvement suggestions.
- Shareable walkthrough links.
- Integrates with GitHub/GitLab.

## Use Cases

- Onboarding reviewers to complex changes.
- Audit trails for compliance.
- Async team feedback loops.
